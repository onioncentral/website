FAQs

What am I actually buying?

A full year's access to Digital ONION® which you can access whenever you want plus 10 layers per month (two full ONION® Runs). Each ONION® Run ends with a summary report you can save and use to help you make progress.

What does “10 layers per month” mean in practice?

A “layer” is one guided step (Outcome, Now, Ideas, Options, Next Steps) in the ONION® process. With 10 layers/month you can typically do two full ONION® Runs (5 layers each), or do one full Run and use remaining layers as you like — it's up to you.

What happens if I want to use it more?

You can buy more layers to top up your account.

What should I bring to an ONION® Run?

Bring one real situation you want greater clarity on — e.g. a decision, a stuck feeling, an opportunity or a challenge.

Examples: “Should I take the job?”, “How do I handle this situation?”, “Do I move / stay?”, “What do I say yes or no to?”

If you're not sure yet, that's fine — the first step helps you name what you actually want. Get clearer now

How long does an ONION® Run take?

Most people complete a full ONION® Run in c.45 minutes. Each layer takes c.8–10 minutes. You can do it all at once or in bits.

Can I stop halfway and come back?

Yes. You can do a layer in one sitting, pause, and return later.

Each layer ends with a Summary Report so you can pick up where you left off — without having to remember what you wrote or start again.

Will this give me answers, or help me find mine?

Digital ONION® is designed to help you find your answers.

It won't try to “tell you what to do” — it guides you through a structured sequence of questions so you can think more clearly, decide what matters, and choose your next step.

What if I don't know exactly what I'm stuck on yet?

Don't worry; that's a common starting point. You can begin with the Outcome layer, which helps you turn a vague sense of “something's off” into a clear, positive outcome you want instead. From there, the rest becomes much easier. Start with Outcome
